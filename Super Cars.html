The Super Cars!

From their names, they are the super, the can do what no other 4 wheel vehicle can. Crazy engines, Crazy performance and breath-taking speeds! The Super Cars are used in track races or displayedin showrooms as a sign of wealth and uniqueness. These cars have strong body with perfect air dynamics, which makes it just break through the air like a plane on 4 wheels. Not many brands are in this field, and many of the super cars are not even availabele commercially.

The World's fastest Super Cars are:

  1. Devel Sixteen — 347 MPH - 558.4 KM/H
    • A V-16 with 3,000 hp

  2. Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut — 330 MPH - 531 KM/H (Sweden)
    • The 5.0-liter twin-turbo V-8

  3. Bugatti Chiron Super Sport — 304.7 MPH - 490.4 KM/H (German)
    • The 1,600 hp, 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged

  4. Hennessey Venom F5 — 300 MPH - 482.8 KM/H (American)
    • A 6.6-liter twin-turbo V-8 pumps out 1,817 hp
