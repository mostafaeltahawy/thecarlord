The Tokyo Monsters!

These cars are very well-known for their extra-ordinary sport ability, they gained the love of youth along the generations. Most of the JDMs are never left as stock, their owners like to modify them to compete. Having record breaking cars and cars that scared competitors on tracks for years, Japanese brands are the #1 car sellers in the whole world.

Brands like Lexus and Infiniti combined luxury and performance. Lexus currently has the fastest JDM car which is the LC500. Japanese brands made way more than just sport cars, those brands made sure to fulfill every person's need of a car.
