The American Beasts!

The Muscle Cars, the name given to the american sport cars of this generation. Thats to mark their strength and power in the market. Very well known for their aggressive, heavy looks; which assembles their vigorous performance. Muscle cars engines are usually huge, and holds very high horsepower. This name came out in the late 20th century, and since then all we see is stronger cars coming from under this name.

The fastest muscle cars are:

  1. Dodge Challenger Demon - 2.3 Seconds 0 to 100 KM/H

  2. 2020 Ford Mustang Shelby GT500 - 3.3 Seconds 0 to 100 KM/H

  3. Dodge Charger SRT Hellcat Widebody - 3.4 Seconds 0 to 100 KM/H
